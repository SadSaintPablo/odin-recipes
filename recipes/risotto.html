Zucchini Risotto!

Learn how to make a delicious Zucchini Risotto that will make you fall in love with this dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 zucchini, chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion, and cook, stirring until onion is translucent.
  2. Stir in rice, and cook until lightly toasted.
  3. Meanwhile, in a medium saucepan, bring chicken broth to a simmer. Slowly add broth to rice, one ladleful at a time, stirring until liquid is absorbed before adding more.
  4. When rice is just tender and all the broth is absorbed, stir in zucchini. Remove from heat, and stir in Parmesan. Serve immediately.
